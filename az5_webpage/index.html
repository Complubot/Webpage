Az5 Robot

A low cost, modular, ROS compliant robotic platform targeted at research in the field of robotics and artificial vision

About AZ5

The robot has an omnidirectional motor platform with mecanum wheels. It is capable of reaching high speeds up to 1 meter per second and carrying up to 50 kg of payload

Numerous sensors are used to recognize the environment and locate the robot in it. The main sensors are an intelRealSense camera, a lidar and a ring of 16 ultrasonic sensors.
